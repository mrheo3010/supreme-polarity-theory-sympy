_SPT Law 52 - Big Bang Singularity Resolution via DA-Cluster Density Bound_

Đợt 22 · v3.24 · 11/05/2026 · Tier A-PASS

Penrose-Hawking singularity theorems (Penrose 1965, Hawking 1967, P-H 1970) prove that under classical GR + energy conditions + global hyperbolicity, spacetime is geodesically incomplete -- i.e., a singularity (infinite density / curvature) must exist in the past of any expanding cosmology. This is the "Big Bang singularity": the universe starts with rho -> oo, T -> oo, R_{mu nu sigma rho} -> oo at t -> 0.
